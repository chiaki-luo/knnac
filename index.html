KNNAC

Notes

  • The L1 speaker speaks the General American Accent
  • Dataset (L2-ARCTIC corpus [1]): https://psi.engr.tamu.edu/l2-arctic-corpus/

Experiment :

  • Input speech: original L2 speech recordings in L2-ARCTIC corpus
  • Target: the L1 speech generated by TTS model FastSpeech2 [2]
  • Baseline1 [3]: A PPG accent conversion method trained on parallel datasets L2-arctic and Arctic
  • Baseline2 [4]: Whisper+FastSpeech2 for the implement of [4]
  • Proposed: the proposed reference-free accent conversion model

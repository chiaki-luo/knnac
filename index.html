TTS-AC: Non-parallel Accent Conversion by Leveraging TTS for prosody modeling

Notes

  • The L1 speaker speaks the General American Accent
  • Dataset (L2-ARCTIC corpus [1]): https://psi.engr.tamu.edu/l2-arctic-corpus/

Experiment :

  • Input speech: original L2 speech recordings in L2-ARCTIC corpus
  • Target: the L1 speech generated by StyleTTS [2] model and Arctic [3]
  • Baseline [4]: A PPG accent conversion method trained on parallel datasets L2-arctic and Arctic
  • Proposed: the proposed reference-free accent conversion model
